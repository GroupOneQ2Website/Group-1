In this section are the things and details in our channel, starting with what streaming tool we will use in live streaming.

Streamlabs OBS

Streamlabs has tools and features for live streamers and content creators. Streaming software, alerts, donations, sponsorship opportunities, stream overlays, and a variety of other tools are available that are designed to help creators monetize their content and increase viewer engagement.
We chose this tool not only because it’s beginner friendly; providing free access and support which on top all of these features, also provides a web version, giving easier access to people - it also has a lot of devices/platforms available for use: Windows, Mac Beta, Android, IOS, and the web version mentioned. Although it has a number of pros, it also has its cons. Streamlabs OBS is usable in a lot of platforms/devices but not all mentioned are fully compatible with it or may not come with native support. Some bugs are also expected to be encountered as it’s open-source. One of the best apps to connect Streamlabs OBS with is YouTube. We have one of the best platforms for streaming and the right tool to use for it.
