Promotional Plan:

One of, if not the easiest, ways our channel can be promoted is through close friends or relatives. Using platforms such as Facebook and YouTube instantly won’t give us as much progress as the algorithm exists. Two of the many factors why the algorithm will make our promotion fail is first: the content we have is not as relevant compared to other contents searched for in the said platforms, second: our channel still doesn't have enough recognition to appear in people’s newsfeeds. Through friends/relatives, much more people will be gained, much more views, likes, and subscribers, giving us just the right amount of power to compete with other related channels.
