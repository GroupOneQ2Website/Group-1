NBA PBA Casual Basketball and School Basketball

Regardless of its set up, basketball would still remain as basketball.
The one and only goal being to shoot the ball inside the ring to score, achieving it through teamwork or solo.
Some games are played one-on-one which are purely based on an individual's skills, and majority are played in teams which mainly focus on coordination with one another.

Basketball is one of the most fluid sports in existence, having different options to choose from place, equipment, and players, it doesn’t always require the actual basketball to play basketball,

the actual court to play at, or even a certain number of people. Not only is basketball fun but it also has some health benefits like every other sport does, both mental and physical benefits.

Some physical and mental benefits from playing basketball are:

  • Strengthening of muscular endurance
  • Building of healthy bones
  • Balance and coordination improvement
  • Lowers stress
  • Boosts self-confidence
Imagine getting healthier but in an enjoyable way, doesn’t it sound like the best deal?
Although fun and very beneficial, sometimes, things in basketball tend to get real messy: injuring another player (foul), taking three or more steps without dribbling (traveling), picking a ball while it's dribbling then dribbling it again (double dribbling), and many more.
